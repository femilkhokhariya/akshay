About the Firm

A single point of contact for every number, filing and registration your business depends on.

Akshay Lukhi & Co. works with individuals, traders and small businesses on the everyday and the official — bookkeeping and returns, GST and audit, insurance and investment planning, and the registrations that let a business legally operate and protect its name.
